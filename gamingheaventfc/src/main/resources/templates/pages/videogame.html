Privacy Policy

Gaming Heaven respects your privacy. We collect only the information needed to provide you with access to features like saving favorites, commenting on games, and customizing your profile.

Information we collect when you register:

  • Nickname
  • Email address
  • Password (stored in encrypted form only)

Optional information you may add later:

  • Profile description
  • Profile picture
  • Favorite games

Information we store through your activity:

  • Games you like or mark as favorites
  • Comments you post on games

We do not collect or track any technical data (like your device, IP address, or browser), and we do not use third-party analytics or monitoring tools.

When you delete your account, all your data — including your profile, favorites, likes, and comments — is permanently removed from our system.

Cookie Policy

Gaming Heaven uses a single cookie to keep you logged in after signing in.

What does this cookie do?
The session cookie allows us to remember your login status while you browse the site. It contains no personal information and is deleted when you log out or close your browser (depending on your settings).

We do not use cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising purposes.
